### Hello 👋 I'm Mark Edward

I have had the opportunity to work on projects with the International Rice Research Institute and the Department of Science and Technology. In 2023, I was conferred the second prize in the [Magsaysay Future Engineers/Technologists Award](https://www.dlsu.edu.ph/ccs-student-bagged-2nd-prize-in-2023-mfet-award/) by the National Academy of Science and Technology of the Philippines.
